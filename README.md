# Superstore_Sales_Data_Analysis
This project analyzes historical sales data from a global retail superstore to understand sales performance across products, regions, and customer segments.

📊 Sales Performance & Profitability Analysis
🧾 Project Overview
The goal is to understand what drives profit, what destroys it, and where the company should focus to improve margins and revenue efficiency.

🎯 Business Problem
Although revenue is growing, profitability remains under pressure. The company faces:
Excessive discounting
Loss-making product categories
Regional performance imbalance
Uneven product profitability

This analysis aims to identify the root causes of profit leakage and recommend actionable strategies.

🛠️ Tools Used
Power BI (Data visualization & dashboarding)
Excel / CSV dataset (data source)
DAX (for calculated measures & KPIs)

📈 Key Business Insights
💥 1. Discounting is destroying profit
No discount orders → ~29.5% margin

40% discount orders → losses up to -120%

~933 orders fall into high-discount loss-making range

👉 Conclusion: Over-discounting is the biggest profit leak.

📉 2. Furniture is a loss-making category

Total sales: $742K
Margin: ~2.5% only
Tables: -8.6% margin (-$17.7K loss)
Bookcases: -3% margin

👉 Furniture is dragging overall profitability.

🚀 3. High-performing products

Paper: 43% margin
Copiers: 37% margin
Phones: 13.49% margin
Accessories: 25.05% margin

👉 These are core profit drivers and should be scaled.

🌍 4. Regional performance gap
West region: 14.9% margin (best)
Central region: 7.9% margin (weakest)
Texas alone: -$25.7K loss

👉 Regional pricing/discounting inconsistency is a major issue.

📊 5. Strong revenue growth, weak margin improvement
Revenue grew 51% (2014 → 2017)
Profit margin remains flat at ~12–13%

👉 Growth is not translating into efficiency.

👥 6. Best customer segment
Home Office: 14% margin (highest)
Corporate: 13%
Consumer: 11.6%

👉 Home Office segment is the most profitable customer base.

💡 Recommendations

🔴 Priority 1: Fix discount strategy
Set maximum discount cap (20%)
Introduce approval rules for high discounts
Stop margin destruction from over-discounting

🔴 Priority 2: Fix Furniture category
Reprice or remove Tables & Bookcases
Review supplier costs
Bundle with high-margin products

🟠 Priority 3: Regional optimization
Investigate Texas, Ohio, Pennsylvania
Analyze discounting + product mix per state

🟢 Priority 4: Scale profitable products
Invest in Paper, Copiers, Phones
Expand marketing for Home Office segment
